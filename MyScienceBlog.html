2-Week Battle Plan

English already done ✓  ·  2 extra days redirected to Maths  ·  weakest subject = most attention

English ✓ Mathematics ×3 days Physics Chemistry Biology
── Week 1 — Maths Blitz
Day 1
Maths extra
Expansion & Factorization — revise all identities: (a+b)², (a-b)², a²-b², (a+b)³
Quadratic Equations by factorization — solve 15 equations, time yourself
💡 Write every identity by hand 3 times. Muscle memory is real.
Day 2
Maths extra
Algebraic Manipulation & Fractions — simplify 15 expressions, focus on LCM of algebraic terms
Simultaneous Equations — 5 questions each: Elimination, Substitution, Graphical
💡 Graphical method: plot carefully, label axes, find intersection point clearly.
Day 3
Maths
Pythagoras Theorem — revise proof + 10 application problems including word problems
Circle: Area & Circumference — 8 problems including composite shapes
Mixed Maths revision — do 10 random questions from all topics covered
💡 This is your third Maths day — by now patterns will start clicking. Trust it.
Day 4
Physics
Gravity, Speed & Motion, Forces & Acceleration — formulas, units, diagrams
Energy — forms, transfer, conservation, GPE ↔ KE calculations
💡 For MCQs: eliminate clearly wrong answers first. Usually 2 options are obviously off.
Day 5
Physics
Magnetism — properties, field diagrams, electromagnets
Electricity — Ohm's Law, series vs parallel circuits, all circuit symbols
Heat Transfer — Conduction, Convection, Radiation with real-life examples
Day 6
Chemistry
Atomic Structure & Periodic Table — protons, neutrons, electrons, periods & groups
Chemical Bonding — ionic vs covalent, dot-cross diagrams
Formulas & Equations — balance 10 equations
Day 7
Rest 🛋️
Light flip through notes only — no new content today
Blue Kernel Labs time 😄 — you earned it
── Week 2 — Lock It In
Day 8
Chemistry
Acids, Alkalis & Salts — pH scale, neutralisation, indicators
Metals: Reactivity Series, Extraction, Corrosion — memorise the reactivity order
Chemicals from Oils & Rate of Reactions — factors affecting rate
Day 9
Biology
Transport in Plants — xylem, phloem, transpiration + factors affecting it
Reproduction in Plants — flower structure, pollination types, fertilization, fruits & seeds
💡 Draw and label diagrams — Bio diagrams are worth marks and help memory.
Day 10
Biology
Control & Coordination — neurons, reflex arc, the eye, hormones
Homeostasis — blood glucose regulation, role of insulin
Diseases & Biotechnology — pathogens, antibiotics, vaccines
Day 11
Maths bonus
Full Maths past paper practice — attempt a full set of questions under 30-min timer
Review every mistake — understand WHY each answer was wrong, not just what was wrong
💡 This bonus day is what separates good from great. Most people skip this step.
Day 12
Revision
Quick-fire review — 20 mins per subject, formulas + key concepts only
Make one A4 cheat sheet per subject — writing it out cements memory
Day 13
Mock Exam
Full mock run — 2:30 hrs, all 5 subjects back to back, real exam conditions
Note every topic where you hesitated or blanked
💡 Most important day of the plan. Simulating exam conditions kills anxiety on the real day.
Day 14
Final Prep
Fix weak spots only — only revisit what tripped you up in the mock
Read through your cheat sheets one last time
Sleep early. A rested brain outperforms a crammed one every single time.
